Overview
Laser Command and Laser Attack are the same exact gear. It seems Astronomical Toys just tried to release it under another name. This gear was pretty cheap and due to it's compatibility with Laser Challenge it was in a pretty good demand.

Gear top
There were two different vests released, and two different types of guns. Unlike the Original Laser Challenge gear, the vests instead of the guns beeped.
