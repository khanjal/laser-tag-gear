Overview
These two gears both have the option of using Team features or the regular features. The Gotcha gear is a more simple version of the Radar gear.

Gear top
The gear listed is good for expanding the V2 arsenal. This gear isn't great by itself.

Gotcha Extreme Mini Mayhem top
Released- 2001
Model Number: 80646T/1
Battery Req.: 3 AAA's
Range: 150 ft.
Ammo: 25
Original Price: ?
Info: This gun/vest set is unique in that the vest can actually clip to the gun. This makes it similar to Tiger Toy's Lazer Tag gear. Mini Mayhem still maintains all the features that the Extreme gear has. I would probably buy one of these sets before a Gotcha set :)

Gotcha Extreme Pistol top
Released- 2000
Battery Req.: 3 AA's
Range: 350 ft.
Ammo: 25
Set(s): Gotcha Extreme Set
Info: This gun is unique in that you can only reload when you are out of ammo. To reload you just tip the gun to the ground and pull the trigger. A very neat idea, if it was used in better equipment. The gun is also single fire only.

Gotcha Extreme Vest top
Released- 2000
Battery Req.: --Needs to be plugged into gun--

Set(s): Gotcha Extreme Set
Info: The vest will only work if it is plugged into the gun. There is no option for the back vest, and when you plug the gun in it enables Max Blasts for the gun. There is team A/B options for the vest and gun too.

Radar Extreme Pistol top
Released- 1999
Battery Req.: 2 AA's
Range: 350 ft.
Ammo: 25
Set(s): Radar Extreme Set
Info: These guns are nice add-on's for V2 gear, but if you only play with Radar Extreme gear, you can use their team features. If you want to play on team A, you hold down the Arm button and turn it on, for team B, you hold down Reset button. If you play in team mode, your gun emits a constant IR that lets your opponent know if you have them targeted. It doesn't let you know you have a lock on them unless you can hear their vest. There is also no front light on this gun.

Radar Extreme Vest top
Released- 1999
Battery Req.: 3 AA's
Accessory Port(s): 1
Set(s): Radar Extreme Set
Info: These sets are basically the same as the V2 vests, but they have the team ability like the Radar Pistols. The Blast Shield button is actually the blue/red circle right on the front, which is sometimes a problem if you lay down you activate your shield. The team A option is enabled by holding in the Blast Shield and turning it on, and the team B option is enabled by holding in the button on the back of the vest. The accessory port on this vest can use an Original Back Vest. This vest also takes hits slower then a V2 vest.
