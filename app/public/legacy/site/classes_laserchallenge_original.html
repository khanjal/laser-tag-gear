Original Laser Challenge Classes
All of these classes uses the Original Laser Challenge Gear. It does not use any of the newer stuff, but you can take these classes and make them to suit your league.

Assault Soldier top
Credit: Maniac
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol & E-XD
Accessories: 1 Laser Trap & Holster for Pistol
Info: Basic Assault Operative. They work best in groups of 1-2. Must be very stealthy.


Attack Spy top
Credit: Brian Caslis
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: None
Info: Sneaks into an area with the regular spies in front of him/her and then attacks!


Bag Soldier top
Credit: Tommy
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: Any and every thing they can use, BAG
Info: Has any weapons or accessories he/she can find or steal from the other team. When people loose their weapons, they come to the Bag Soldier and get new weapons. The Demolition Soldiers will use them a lot.


Body Guard top
Credit: Tony Schraeder
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol and EX-D Rifle
Accessories: None
Info: A Body Guard's job is to protect the Commander, with his/her life if it comes to it. He/she also supervises the Commander to see how good his plans are.


Bomb Squad top
Credit: Manta_X
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): 2 Pistols
Accessories: None
Info: This is a way to get rid of Laser Traps and stinky players! If an enemy sets a trap... make the Bomb Squad pick it up and run with it! Although he/she may die, it's better than letting a more experienced player die!


Bomber top
Credit: Matt
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: Laser Traps
Info: Uses Laser Traps and kills.


Bounty Hunter top
Credit: PhazerStorm
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): 2 Modified Blasters and 1 Heavily Modified E-XD
Accessories: 3-4 Laser Traps
Info: The Blasters, E-XD, front, and back vests should be silenced and painted black. The bounty hunter runs into combat immediately when the game starts, and takes out as many people as possible.


Captor top
Credit: BornToBattle
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D Rifle
Accessories: None
Info: This person captures enemies, when recon is needed. They try not to kill the person, but wound them until they have 1 shot left.


Combat Medic top
Credit: Maniac
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: Optional Holster, Walkie Talkie
Info: Combat Medics are the only team members that can reset vests. They should carry a walkie talkie so they can talk to other members that need to be revived. They can defend themselves with pistols. They are a great target because once they die, there is no one to revive people.


Commander top
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D Rifle
Accessories: Laser Trap
Info: Your Commander is your leader. He/She makes the strategies and issues the orders. Protect him/her at will for he/she is valuable.


Commando top
Credit: Tiberium
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): 2 EX-D Rifles
Accessories: 3 Laser Traps
Info: (Be sure to have something to keep the traps in). Ambushes snipers and demo solders.


Counter Sniper top
Credit: Chris Kelley
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D
Accessories: None
Info: Finds and destroys Snipers in teams of 2.

Demolition Soldier top
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): None
Accessories: Laser Traps (as many as he/she can hold, at least 4)
Info: Armed with only Laser Traps, the Demo Soldier should have an escort just in case he/she runs into trouble. His/her main job is to, before an assault begins, to strategically throw all of his/her Laser Traps into the enemy's base, hopefully confusing them and making them scatter.


Demolitions Specialist top
Credit: Maniac
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol w/holster
Accessories: 2+ Laser Traps
Info: Demolitions Specialists set and disarm bombs. They will most likely die, but they protect their team from bombs though.


Decoy Soldier top
Credit: Tommy
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: 1 Laser Trap
Info: When battle starts the Decoy Soldiers run toward the other teams base. The enemy's will focus on them and your other Soldiers can attack.


Doom Soldier top
Credit: Blacklotus
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): 2 Pistols ,2 EX-D Rifles and 1 Micro Blaster
Accessories: 5 Laser Traps
Info: His/her job is when his/her side is losing, to go and waste everyone he/she can.


Fragger top
Credit: Detonator
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol or Micro Blaster
Accessories: Belt
Info: A fragger is needed in capture the flag Laser Tag games. A Fragger NEEDS escort by at least 2 soldiers. The idea of the Fragger is to snatch the flag while the enemy is dealing with his/her escort(s), and to be able to run as fast as he/she can back to base safely with the flag tucked away in a belt around his/her waist.


General top
Credit: Maniac
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: Optional Holster
Info: A General commands the teams forces. They make plans and execute orders. They should be protected well, a General only gets one life. Once he is dead, game over. He should run around and hide a lot, and he should look like all other players.


Guard/Sentry top
Credit: Tim Prince
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D Rifle
Accessories: None
Info: A Guards only job is protecting the main base, the Guard should stand/sit almost completely still, although if he/she hears leafs rustling or twigs snapping the Guard should get up and look around. It may seem kind of boring just standing around, but a Guard is a necessary.


Heavy Weapons Soldier top
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Dual EX-D Rifles
Accessories: None
Info: Armed with not one but two EX-D's, a Heavy Weapons soldier has a duty to back-up the regular soldiers. Theoretically he/she could fire in two directions at once maybe giving enough cover to the regular soldiers if they needed to retreat.


Infiltrator/Spy top
Equipment: Micro Gun Vest
Weapon(s): Micro Gun
Accessories: Walkie-Talkie
Info: The Infiltrator/Spy should keep an eye on the enemy base most if not all of the time. He/She should be very well hidden so they do not see him/her. As soon as he/she sees them getting ready to attack or do something else that might be considered important enough, he/she should contact the Info-Relay Soldier, and he/she should warn the Commander.


Information Relay Soldier top
Credit: Tommy
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: Walkie-Talkie
Info: These Soldiers get information from the Spy(s) and relay it to the Commander.


The Insider top
Credit: Ricky
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol and Micro Gun
Accessories: None
Info: The Insider will hide the Micro Gun in his/her pocket and then surrender. While the Insider isn't under surveillance he/she will pull out the Micro Gun and get as many shots on anybody that's around or shoot before they plan to kill him/her.


Lunatic top
Credit: Alex
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Anything that they can carry
Accessories: As many Laser Traps as possible
Info: This is probably the funniest class. You are a lone shark. All you do is go after both teams with your bombs and guns, or can make an alliance with one and demolish one. Fun heh?


Officer top
Credit: The Grove
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: Walkie Talkie
Info: This person is the next rank lower to the Commander. He/She is needed for bigger games with many players. His/Her job is to be like the Commander, only he/she should be with a different group than the Commander. You should give the rifles to the Soldiers, not the Officers. Also if your team is about ready to lose, then you can have the Officer be a back-up soldier.


Recon/Infiltration Operative top
Credit: Maniac
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol & Micro Blaster
Accessories: None
Info: Recon Operatives are meant to attack the base with some cover, to make the operation look normal. Once they get hit plenty, they surrender. The guards will take his pistol, and his micro blaster should be hidden in a large pocket. He should listen in and get info on the enemy. Once he isn't under watch, he should escape, using the micro blaster if he has to. Then he reports back to base. Skilled Recon specialists are usually successful, unless they enemy searches the person and takes their micro blaster.


Rifleman top
Credit: The Grove
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D Rifle
Accessories: Heavy Camouflage, and 1 or 2 Laser Traps
Info: This person is mainly needed for capture the flag games. His job is to sneak around in the woods and look for the flag or the enemy base. Once he finds it he lay the laser traps where the enemy base is. He also can be a main assault trooper.


Scout top
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: None
Info: The Scout's job is to patrol along your bases perimeter making sure everything is ok.


Secret Soldier top
Credit: Blacklotus
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Modified Pistol and Pistol
Accessories: Walkie Talkie ,Laser Trap, and whatever
Info: The modified pistol has a Micromax blaster 1/5 the size, a Laser Pointer, and Hunting Scope. They hide until told to move in. Until the other side thinks that your team is losing, and you are out of people, come out of hiding and try to kill every one on the other team still alive.


Sniper top
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D Rifle (with foam taped over the speaker slots to deafen the sound)
Accessories: None
Info: The Sniper's mission is to attack the enemy base from a great distance. With his/her weapon's sound lower than normal, the enemy might not know where it is coming from. Normally an attack by the Sniper shouldn't start until either side is starting to move. Here is a great Instruction Guide made by Nic Bertino.


Sniper, S.E.A.L. top
Credit: Nic Bertino
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): E-XD
Accessories: Grappling Hook, Foot Spikes, Hand Claws, Tree Stands
Info: A Sniper (a good one) should stay alone ALWAYS. A Sniper should be your BEST player, and have great marksmanship. Acting out of hiding, a sniper should be quick and stealthy. Proper tactics MUST be used in order for a Sniper to be effective. Here are the Sniper rules: 1. Don't get caught. 2. Don't get caught. 3. Don't get CAUGHT!


Soldier top
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D Rifle
Accessories: None
Info: Of all the classes, the Soldiers should be the most common. The Soldiers are a team's main army. They are given orders from the Commander and should follow them without question.


Special Tactic Unit top
Credit: Xtreme
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D (modified), Poppers
Accessories: Walkie Talkie
Info: This is a unit that goes into the enemy's base without being noticed. The gun must be silenced. They are the best of the best. The poppers are for a distraction, while they find the flag.


Squad Leader top
Credit: Capt.Moore
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D blaster, (Laser Pistol at his/her side)
Accessories: 1 Laser Trap
Info: Squad Leader controls 3 soldiers. He/she is rank under the First Officer (Commander, First officer, Squad leader). Squad leader take orders from them. For instance if the commander wanted to attack from two sides, this is where the Squad Leader would come in.


Standard Gunman/Soldier top
Credit: Maniac
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol
Accessories: Optional Holster
Info: The name says it. These guys are the rookies. They work best in teams of 2-4.


Stealth Sniper top
Credit: Maniac
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): E-XD Modified, explained below
Accessories: Binoculars (optional), cammo clothes
Info: A Stealth Sniper is a stealthy sniper. They must move quickly and quietly. Their E-XD should be modified with a mute button and extra range attachments. E-mail on how to make these mods. They sniper from long range and should have a lot of cammo on. A good spot for them is in a tree or in a ditch.


Suicide Bomber top
Credit: Jabberwocky
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D Rifle
Accessories: 1 Laser Trap, Death Wish (Figuratively)
Info: This one carries the Demolition Soldier one step further... a Demo Soldier that PERSONALLY ENSURES HIS/HER BOMB HITS PEOPLE! Tripping his/her bomb and running into crowds, he/she does some damage and does it better because there is a very slim margin for failure.


Suicide Soldier top
Credit: Jabberwocky
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): EX-D Rifle
Accessories: 1-2 Laser Traps
Info: Runs into the enemy base the second the game starts and shoots as many people as possible. Takes down the enemy for the sane people. THEIR MISSION PROFILE IS TO DIE! Sometimes they win the game.

Tactical Ops Squad top
Credit: Maniac
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Varies for Assault, Demo and Recon, read below
Accessories: Varies for Assault, Demo and Recon, read below
Info: The name explains them. Tactical Ops Teams contain of Assault experts, Demo (Demolitions) experts, and Recon/Infiltration. Assault members take: 1 E-XD, 1 Pistol w/ holster, & 1 Laser Trap. They do frontal attacks and sneak up attacks. They should be stealthy. They work in groups of 2-4. They also attack snipers. Demo members should take: 1 Ultrawide w/ strap, 2-4 Laser Traps, and a backpack to carry Laser Traps in. Their job is to attack large groups of people plus arm & disarm Laser Traps. They work best in a group of 2. Recon members should have: 1 Micro blaster. Hide it in a pocket. Recon members gather enemy info of any type. They should be VERY stealthy. They also move in after Assault or Demo teams and pick up any lost weapons. They work best in split up groups of 2.


Techno Warrior top
Credit: roverT*
Equipment: Front and Back Vest
Weapon(s): Pistol "modified"
Accessories: None
Info: A Techno Warrior is devoted to the game and will use any fair and true way to win. With a series of modifications on his gun, "such as a laser rifle modified to fit inside of the already existing gun" and remotes on the sides of the gun. He/She will always strive to fight back and win.

Warlord top
