Overview
Laser Challenge V2 is the best Laser Challenge gear made. It is very durable and has well rounded features. It was also very easy to acquire and didn't cost an arm and a leg. A lot of the V2 gear went on clearance at most Toys R' Us's in 2000. I bought a few sets when they were selling real low.

Gear top
There are a few options to the V2 line. There are 3 guns, and all vests come with a build in back vest. ELS adapters and computers give some neat features too. All of the guns feature Max Blasts, which is 3 hits for one shot if it hits the opponent, and the ability to turn the gun on with or without sound (first introduced on Micromax Blasters). All of the vests contain Blast Shields, which are 10 second invulnerability. You start out with 3 Max Blasts and 3 Blast Shields when you activate the Vest and Gun.

ELS Computer top
Released- 1998
Battery Req.: 3 AA's
Accessory Port(s): 3; 2 gun and 1 Parallel
Set(s): ELS Game System
Info: The ELS computer adds Laser Challenge Pro like features to the V2 line. When you first turn it on you can program the time limit (up to 10 hours), number of hits (up to 30), and how many are on each team (red and blue). Each person plugs in when it asks and everyone gets a unique ID. When the game is over, whether time ran out or one whole team is dead, everyone plugs back in and it pulls up various statistics like hit % for each person, who killed who, the best shooter, and the worst shooter. You cannot shoot your teammates or yourself and you have a limited amount of reloads. You just plug back into the computer to ge t more reloads. The only down side is there can only be a maximum of 8 people.
There is also ELS software to connect with a computer and print out the results.

Firestorm top
Released- 1998
Model Number: 80613
Battery Req.: 6 AA's (take out front 3 to disable recoil)
Range: 1000 ft.
Ammo: 25
Original Price: $29.99
Info: This is one of the best Laser Challenge guns out there. With an insane range on firing, it takes a while to learn how to aim this beast correctly. It contains a scope that assists you in aiming. Omega was constructed out of a Firestorm.

Ultrawide top
Released- 1998
Model Number: 80614
Battery Req.: 3 AA's
Range: 350 ft./Max spread 50 ft. wide
Ammo: 25 Normal/1 shot every 5 seconds on Ultrawide
Original Price: $29.99
Set(s): Ultrawide Blaster Set
Info: This is a nice rifle style gun. It has a nice aiming scope and is very easy to aim. You can also activate Ultrawide mode, which spreads the barrels and gives you a small area that you can hit people. This mode is hard to use and normal mode is preferred by most.

V2 Vest top
Released- 1998
Battery Req.: 3 AA's
Accessory Port(s): 1
Set(s): Solo Gear, Twin Gear, and Ultrawide Blaster Set
Info: The V2 Vests are nice in that they take hits quickly (10 hits in about 6 seconds) and they have elastic bands to keep them positioned properly. The back vest is hard wired to the front vest, and the port on the front vest is for the ELS system. There is a 9th hit sound which gives you about 3 seconds, and a 10th hit sound which makes it known you are dead.
