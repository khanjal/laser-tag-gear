LAZER TAG TEAM OPS FROM HASBRO IS THE ULTIMATE LAZER TAG EXPERIENCE

LAZER TAG TEAM OPS Combines Extreme Team Play with Video Game Play For Cool Competition and Sport 



PAWTUCKET, RI – February 15, 2004 – “Tag, you’re it!,” takes on an extreme new meaning with LAZER TAG TEAM OPS from Hasbro, Inc.’s (NYSE: HAS) Tiger Electronics brand. This ‘ultimate Lazer Tag experience’ features a unique Heads Up Display (HUD) system and Tagger ID so it’s easy to proclaim the Tag Master after every game.


LAZER TAGTEAM OPS combines hands-on extreme team play with a unique video game play-like feature to deliver a one-of-a-kind lazer tag experience.


The LAZER TAG TEAM OPS DELUXE 2 PLAYER SET features the unique Heads Up Display and Tagger system that provides audio and visual confirmation whenever you accurately ‘tag’ an enemy and lets you know if you’ve ‘locked in’ your target – or if your target has got a ‘lock’ on you!


The innovative LAZER TAG TEAM OPS HUD HEADSET and Tagger system plays high quality sound effects and features Tagger ID, an LCD screen that displays individual and team statistics after each game play. It also lets you find out who tagged who and how many times a player has been tagged. Now you can really know what team is best and determine every game’s Tag Master!


The LAZER TAG TEAM OPS DELUXE 2 PLAYER SYSTEM (ages 8 years and up) comes with two Taggers and two HUDs (Heads Up Displays). The set retails for approximately $59.99 and will be available at retail nationwide in September 2004.


With LAZER TAG TEAM OPS, three teams of eight players can play eleven preprogrammed games like ‘Own the Zone,’ and ‘Capture the Flag.’ There are also eight customizable game features for virtually endless game play. And, a special communications feature lets you contact team members during play.


The awesome new LAZER TAG TEAM OPS TEAM TALK HEADSET enhances team play. The three-channel, two-way walkie-talkie system attaches to the Heads Up Display and allows you to communicate with your teammates – or your enemy. With its long range, the TEAM OPS TEAM TALK HEADSET takes the game to a new level of strategic play.


The LAZER TAG TEAM OPS TEAM TALK HEADSET (ages 8 years and up) includes two TEAM TALK HEADSETS; each headset comes on a carabineer clip that affixes easily to any belt loop. The unit also features an earpiece with microphone so players can be ‘in the know’ when they are ‘on the go’ in search of their target. 


The LAZER TAG TEAM OPS TEAM TALK HEADSET will retail for approximately $ 24.99 and will be available nationwide in September 2004.
