Guns
We use V2 compatible guns mainly. We prefer V2 guns to the small variety at our disposal, for those who like their preferences. Any guns that break are not a big worry. We have enough for two guns to one person.

Vests top
We use V2 vests for our main choice. Due to the problem if we loose a vest we have one less person that can play, we value the vests more so then the guns. The vests take 10 hits and have 3 5-sec blast shields.
