Laser Commandos Games
This is a list of what games we have played from 2000 on. The list includes the dates, start and end times, locations, and participants (total number in blue, their arrival, and their departure). I attend all games from start to finish, but do not have my name listed. If there was a special game or something memorable happened, it's name/description will be right below the location in red and smaller lettering.


Laser Commandos Extreme Games
Extreme games are classified if it is raining, snowing, or below 40 degrees Fahrenheit. The games are signified by the yellow X image that appears with the temperature beside it in red.


Laser Commandos Game Pics
Sometimes during some games, pictures are taken. If they are suitable enough, they are put as links at the end of the list of names. I try to remember to take pics at most games, but sometimes I forget.
