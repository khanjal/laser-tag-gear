Laser Challenge V2 ELS Classes
These classes use the V2 ELS system. Basically it's two teams, red/blue, battling against each other. You can only use ELS gear for these classes.

Base Commander top
Credit: Bryan
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): V2 Pistol
Accessories: ELS Unit, Walkie Talkie
Info: This class uses his/her ElS to give teammates needed reloads, and relay info to other players.


Captain top
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): Ultrawide Rifle
Accessories: None
Info: He should be a good back-up for the Commander.


Commander top
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): Firestorm
Accessories: None
Info: The Commander is the heart of the team. He figures out how to win. He can also come up with some good strategies while under pressure.


Demolition Soldier top
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): Ultrawide
Accessories: None
Info: Unlike the Demolition Soldiers using the other gear, he/she uses an Ultrawide. He/she should be an expert at the Ultrawide. You can tell, because they can get it to rapid fire while the gun is in Ultrawide mode (yes it is possible). He/she keeps the enemy base under fire causing havoc.


Heavy Weapons Soldier top
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): Firestorm
Accessories: None
Info: A Heavy weapons soldiers job is to back up other soldiers. Using his/her precision aiming ability, they can take down anyone at a long distance.


Infiltrator/Spy top
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): V2 Pistol
Accessories: Walkie Talkie
Info: Armed with only a pistol the Infiltrator/Spy is to keep a strong eye on the enemy's base. He/she reports to the Commander.


Scout top
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): V2 Pistol
Accessories: None
Info: The Scout patrols the base perimeter. Sometimes the Scout will go into the enemy's or just wonder around looking for trouble.


Sniper top
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): Firestorm
Accessories: None
Info: Armed with the longest range weapon made so far, the Sniper's job is to shoot from as far away as possible. They should be good shots and a master of stealth. They have the gun sounds off. Here is a great Instruction Guide made by Nic Bertino.


Soldier top
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): Ultrawide
Accessories: None
Info: Basic Laser Challenge class. The Soldier is an all around unit. Can be given a specific job if necessary, but that's what the other classes are for.


Spy Guard top
Credit: Brian Caslis
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): V2 Pistol
Accessories: None
Info: This class simply guards the Spy.


Super Weapons Soldier top
Credit: Bryan/Warrior
Equipment: V2 Front and Back Vest, Red/Blue ELS Adapter
Weapon(s): 2 Firestorms
Accessories: ELS Unit, Walkie Talkie
Info: This is a Soldier who carries the most powerful weapons, Firestorms. The Captain or Commander sends him to do the dirty work, and take out all the players still alive.
