Omega
Omega is my pride and joy Laser Challenge Gun. I wanted a good weapon, and one that stood out that was the best, so I made Omega. It has taken years to get her to this state, but right now she stands as the most powerful weapon in my arsenal.
Info
Class Type Modified Firestorm
Given Name Omega
First Modified August 1999
--Features Added
  1. Recoil Maker removed (rewired battery compartment to provide power to another V2 system)
  2. 1x scope (drilled out two top screw holes, and secured it by plastic wraps)
  3. V2 pistol system (took a pistol apart, cut off and sodered arm, trigger, and on/off switches to new ones, hot glued pistol barrel right above firestorm barrel)
  4. Painted Omega Symbol (Model paint and a computer print out off the symbol made into a pattern tracer)
Second Modification July 17, 2000
--Features Added
  1. 2nd Pistol IR lens (took a nonworking pistol's barrel and added it to the current one)
  2. Ultrawide IR lenses added (took both barrels and added them to the current network of IR lenses)
Third Modification November 12 - December 21, 2000
--Features Added
  1. Repaired Minor Malfunctions
  2. Removed all extra barrels and added only the two Pistol barrels, and one Gotcha Barrel
  3. Rewired network of extra barrels.
  4. Added Laser Pointer
Fourth Modification January 26, 2001
--Features Added
  1. Hot glued flashlight to gun.
Fifth Modification November 17, 2001
--Features Added
  1. Repaired and Rewired Omega's malfunctioning parts.
Planned Future Modifications
  1. Camera Mount (pinhole cam and a wireless transmitter, estimated cost: around $300. It'll be awhile for that one)
  2. Gun Stand (to keep the gun from laying on the glued barrels)
